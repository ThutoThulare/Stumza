THE ENTITLEMENT LIST

A CHRONICLE OF "ROYAL" ARROGANCE

Entitlement isn't just about money; it's the belief that the rules don't apply to you because of the crest on your chest.

01. THE FIGO "KIDNAPPING"

Year: 2000

Real Madrid wanted Barcelona’s captain. They didn't just bid for him; Florentino Perez tricked Luís Figo into a pre-contract during an election. It remains the ultimate "we take what we want" moment in football history, showing zero respect for the biggest rivalry in the world.

ARROGANCE LEVEL: 10/10

02. WHISTLING THE LEGENDS

Ongoing Tradition

The Bernabéu fans have whistled Cristiano Ronaldo, Iker Casillas, and Gareth Bale—players who won them countless trophies. Why? Because they weren't "entertained" for 10 minutes. It’s the peak of entitlement: demanding perfection from legends as if they are circus performers.

LOYALTY LEVEL: 0/10

03. THE SUPER LEAGUE COUP

Year: 2021 - Present

Florentino Perez tried to destroy the Champions League to create a "closed shop" where Madrid would never have to worry about missing out on money. The reasoning? "To save football." Translation: "To save our bank account so we can keep buying everyone else's best players."

EGO LEVEL: OFF THE CHARTS

04. THE MBAPPÉ SAGA

Year: 2022 - 2024

For three years, Madrid acted as if Kylian Mbappé was already their property. When he initially stayed at PSG, the Madrid press acted like a crime had been committed against the King. They believe every top player is simply "on loan" to other clubs until Madrid decides to collect them.

PATIENCE LEVEL: NON-EXISTENT
