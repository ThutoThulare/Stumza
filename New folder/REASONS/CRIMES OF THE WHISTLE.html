CRIMES OF THE WHISTLE

WARNING: The following content may cause extreme frustration for fans of fair play.
2024

The Almería VAR Leak

Bottom-of-the-league Almería was leading 2-0 at the Bernabéu. Then, VAR happened. A controversial penalty for Madrid, a disallowed goal for Almería, and a Vini Jr. handball that was somehow ruled a "shoulder." The leaked audio showed officials leading the ref to the "right" decision.

2018

The "Trash Can" Heart (vs Juventus)

After Juve pulled off a miracle 3-0 comeback, Michael Oliver awarded a 93rd-minute penalty to Madrid. Gianluigi Buffon's reaction—saying the ref had a "trash can for a heart"—became legendary. Madrid moved on; football lost its soul.

2017

The Bayern Munich Robbery

Two offside goals by Cristiano Ronaldo were allowed to stand, and Arturo Vidal was sent off for a tackle where he clearly won the ball. One of the most statistically impossible officiating displays in UCL history.

2026

The 9-Minute Replay

A recent match against Rayo Vallecano saw the referee add 9 minutes of "ghost" time, eventually awarding a penalty in the 98th minute to secure a Madrid win when the clock should have been long dead.
